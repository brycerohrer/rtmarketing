Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is your hourly rate?

    Our hourly rate is dependent on the project size and required allocation of resources, you can contact us for more pricing information.

  • How is this different from paying-by-project?

    We adjust the pricing method specifically to your businessess needs and don't allow paying-by-project because we want to make sure you are getting the service you deserve.

  • How do your payment terms work?

    We must recieve 50% payment before we begin the project, the other 50% can be deposited after you are done. Each customer gets 2 revision rounds and refunds are not allowed.

    We do not hold contracts for our monthly marketing plans, so at the end of each month you can change your strategy with RT Marketing.
